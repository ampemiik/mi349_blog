The Little Things

About the Author: Miika Ampe

Hi, I’m Miika Ampe! I am 21 years old and am from Boston, Massachusetts. At the moment, I am a student at Michigan State University to get my bachelor’s degree in Information Science with a focus in Human-Centered Technologies. I am very interested in the tech industry as a whole, but am especially interested in app, software, and web development and how they could better serve users.

Some of my favorite things to do in my free time include:

  • Skateboarding
  • Gymnastics
  • Listening to music
  • Collecting physical media
  • Spending time with my friends
  • Playing video games on my Wii

My biggest goals for my future are to travel and see the world, create digital platforms and interfaces for users, and learn more about the world of tech. My dream would be to find an online job, whether I employ myself or work for another company, so that I can travel and create at the same time. The world could be my office. In the meantime, I am really enjoying my time in college learning about programming, technology, and media so I can help make my dreams a reality.
