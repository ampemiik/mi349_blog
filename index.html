Author: Miika Ampe

hello there! This is a picture of a squirrel giving a side-eye glance. Squirrels are known for their agility and quick movements, often seen darting around trees and gathering food. They are a common sight on many campuses, where they adapt well to urban environments. This particular squirrel seems to be observing its surroundings with a curious and cautious demeanor.
